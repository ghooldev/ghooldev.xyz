Downloading Our Software

Getting our sofware downloaded and installed on your PC, is a lot easier than you may think (once you understand how to do it)

Here are the steps to follow:

    1. Go to the Ghool Shop
    2. Download the tool titled "ghool@INSTALLS"
    3. Run the tool that you downloaded.
    4. Pick what you want to install from the list.
    5. Wait until it is done, and you have got the tool!
